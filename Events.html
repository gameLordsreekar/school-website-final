Intra School Sports Inauguration
DATE: 25-11-2019

“The thing that sets champions apart from everyone else is the work ethics and the mental fortitude to succeed.” The Intra School Sports inauguration ceremony was conducted on 25th November, 2019. Shri. Harish, Chariman, Vagdevi Vilas Institution presided over the function.
The School Flag was hoisted by the Chairman and the march past led by the primary leaders boosted the mettle and morale of the students. The Chairman spoke about the
importance of healthy mind, body and practicing sports mainly in life too. He stressed on a perfect balance of work and play. The mass drill of Std 1 & 2 and the inaugural dance by Std 3 & 4 added onto the zeal and enthusiasm of students.
The torch of the Sports Meet was lit and the Chairman formally inaugurated the Sports Meet by playing one over cricket match.



Children’s Day Celebration
DATE: 14-11-2019

“Every child is a different kind of flower and children together make this world a beautiful garden.”
Children’s Day was celebrated on 14th November, 2019 by the staff and students of Vagdevi Vilas School.
The assembly which was conducted by the teachers was well appreciated and the staff put up a variety of
programmes to entertain the students. The Chief Guest of the day, Shri. K Harish, Chairman VVI, spoke about the positive nature of teachers and
emphasized on the fact that the motivation of teachers plays a significant role for achieving success in life.
The Chairman highlighted childhood as a stage of playfulness and advised the children to channelize their positive energy effectively.
The celebration ignited the significance thought that children carry the hopes of brighter tomorrow and dreams of a happy future.
